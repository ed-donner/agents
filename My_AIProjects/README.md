# Windowolf Chatbot

Professional window cleaning business chatbot with AI evaluation and lead capture.

## Environment Variables Required:
- OPENAI_API_KEY
- GOOGLE_API_KEY (for Gemini evaluation)
- PUSHOVER_TOKEN
- PUSHOVER_USER

## Features:
- AI-powered customer service
- Lead capture with contact information
- Quality control with Gemini evaluation
- Pushover notifications for job leads
