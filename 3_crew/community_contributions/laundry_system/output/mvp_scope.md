```markdown
# Laundry Business MVP Feature List

## Customer App
### Features
- **User Registration & Authentication** (Must)
  - Email registration and login
  - Social media login options
- **Service Selection & Pricing** (Must)
  - Choose from available laundry services (wash, dry, fold, etc.)
  - Transparent pricing display
- **Order Placement** (Must)
  - Schedule pickup and drop-off times
  - Select specific services for items
- **Real-time Order Tracking** (Should)
  - Provide updates on order status
- **Payment Integration** (Must)
  - Support for credit/debit cards and online wallets
- **Feedback & Rating System** (Should)
  - Customers can rate their service and provide comments.

### In-Scope
- Basic customer feature set to initiate orders.
- Essential user engagement through ratings and reviews.

### Out-of-Scope
- Advanced loyalty programs.
- Referral systems.

## Staff Tools
### Features
- **Order Management Dashboard** (Must)
  - View and manage incoming orders
- **Real-time Notifications** (Must)
  - Alerts for new orders, pickups, and drop-offs
- **Status Update Functionality** (Should)
  - Ability to update order status (in-progress, completed, picked-up, etc.)
- **Analytics Dashboard** (Later)
  - Overview of daily orders, peak times, and operational metrics.

### In-Scope
- Essential tools for staff to manage daily operations and customer orders.

### Out-of-Scope
- Advanced analytics or reporting beyond daily order summary.
- Staff performance metrics.

## Admin Panel
### Features
- **User Management** (Must)
  - Manage customer accounts and permissions
- **Service Management** (Must)
  - Add, update, or remove services and pricing
- **Order Analytics Overview** (Should)
  - View order volumes, revenue, and customer activity
- **Issue Tracking** (Later)
  - Log and track customer complaints or issues.

### In-Scope
- Basic admin features to manage users, services, and initial insights into operations.

### Out-of-Scope
- Advanced analytics tools with predictive modeling.
- Complex reporting features and integrations with third-party services.

## System Constraints
- Must be mobile-responsive for customer app.
- Adherence to GDPR and other relevant data protection regulations.
- Payment processing must comply with PCI-DSS standards.

```

This MVP feature list is designed to provide a focused scope that will enable the initial launch of the laundry business within 14 days, ensuring a user-friendly interface for customers while efficiently equipping staff and administrators to handle operations effectively.
