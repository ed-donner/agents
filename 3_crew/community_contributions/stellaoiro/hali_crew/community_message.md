Dear Parents,  

I understand that you might be worried about the HPV vaccine and if it could affect your daughter's future ability to have children. It's a common concern, but I want to reassure you. Many studies have shown that there is no link between the HPV vaccine and infertility. In fact, it has been proven safe and effective in preventing cervical cancer. The World Health Organization and our own Kenya Ministry of Health support this, emphasizing that the benefits of this vaccine far outweigh any unfounded fears.  

By vaccinating your daughter, you are protecting her health and future. Let’s keep our girls safe and healthy together!  

Chanjo salama. Binti yako anastahili.
