**Chosen Company for Investment: Fal.ai**

**Reason for Selection:**
Fal.ai has been chosen for investment due to its notable position in the rapidly expanding multimodal AI infrastructure sector. The company has demonstrated a swift growth trajectory, seen through its successful fundraising round of $250 million, which increased its valuation beyond $4 billion. Fal.ai is poised to greatly benefit from the growing dependence on AI technologies across various industries, aligning with a market expected to reach $4.8 trillion by 2033. Its robust potential for revenue growth, indicated by a rise to $200 million by 2025 from $25 million in 2024, and strategic innovations make it a compelling investment opportunity.
