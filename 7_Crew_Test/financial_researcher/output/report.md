## 1. Current Company Status and Health
Apple Inc. continues to be a major player in the technology sector, with notable financial metrics as of Q4 2023:
- **Revenue**: The company reported quarterly revenue of $89.5 billion, down 1% year over year. The annual revenue for fiscal 2023 was approximately $383.2 billion.
- **Gross Margin**: Apple maintained a solid gross profit margin of 44.1%, demonstrating operational efficiency.
- **Earnings**: Earnings per diluted share were $1.46 for the quarter, reflecting a significant 13% increase from the previous year.
- **Financial Health**: The company is currently experiencing its longest revenue slide in over two decades, prompting a strategic shift towards diversifying production away from China to mitigate risks.
