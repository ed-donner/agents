# Crisis and support (static reference)

**This app is not for emergencies.** It does not provide clinical care or crisis counselling.

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact **local emergency services** (for example **911** in the United States or  **112** in Nigeria).

## Suicide Research and Prevention Initiative (SURPIN) — 09034400009  Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

## Finding help outside the US

- Search for your country’s **crisis hotline** or **emergency number** from a trusted government or health ministry website.

## Ongoing mental health care

For diagnosis or treatment, work with a **licensed clinician** or your **primary care provider**. This tool is only for **wellness routines, journaling, and general psychoeducation** from allowlisted sources.
