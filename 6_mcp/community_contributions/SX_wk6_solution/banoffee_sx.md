# Banoffee Pie Recipe

## Ingredients
- **For the crust:**
  - 250g digestive biscuits
  - 125g butter, melted

- **For the filling:**
  - 397g can of caramel (or dulce de leche)
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 300ml double cream
  - 1 tbsp icing sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract

- **For the topping:**
  - Grated chocolate or cocoa powder

## Method
1. **Prepare the crust:**
   - Crush the digestive biscuits. Mix them with melted butter and press into the base of a pie dish. Chill in the fridge for about 30 minutes.

2. **Add caramel:**
   - Spread the caramel over the biscuit base and slice the bananas on top, spreading them evenly.

3. **Make the topping:**
   - Whip the double cream with icing sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form. Spread it over the bananas.

4. **Garnish:**
   - Top with grated chocolate or sift cocoa powder over.

5. **Chill:**
   - Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving. Enjoy!
