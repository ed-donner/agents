# MODE Aquarium - Business Information

## About MODE Aquarium

MODE Aquarium designs and manufactures premium aquarium systems for residential, commercial, and institutional customers. The company specializes in high-quality, durable aquarium systems that combine functionality, aesthetics, and ease of maintenance.

MODE Aquarium serves:

- Home aquarium enthusiasts
- Independent fish stores
- Aquarium retailers
- Pet stores
- Public aquariums
- Schools and universities
- Zoos
- Museums
- Commercial facilities
- Interior designers

The company provides both standard aquarium systems and fully customized solutions.
