* premium tech newsletter aesthetic
* dark-mode-first
* modern
* minimal
* editorial

* professional

Critical Rules:

  * td
  * div
  * span
  * p
  * h1-h4
  * a
  * button

  * padding
  * font-size

* flexbox
* CSS grid
* JavaScript

* gradients
* transparency
